Peach Kuchen

Description: A plate of a rich German-Texas peach kuchen.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups unbleached all-purpose organic flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup whold milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

For the topping:

  • 5 ripe peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Cut in the cold, cubed butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually add the milk, mixing until the dough comes together. Do not overmix.
  5. Press the dough into the bottom of a greased 9x13-inch baking pan to form a thin crust.
  6. Arrange the sliced peaches evenly over the dough.
  7. In a small bowl, mix together the sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Sprinkle this mixture over the peaches.
  8. Drizzle the melted butter over the peaches and sugar mixture.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the peaches are tender.
  10. Allow the peach kuchen to cool slightly before serving. It can be served warm or at room temperature.
