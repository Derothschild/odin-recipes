Spicy Potato Soup

Description: A bowl of hearty German-Texas spicy potato soup.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cups of potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the diced potatoes to the pot and sauté for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth, and add the bay leaf, ground mustard, ground paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Cover the pot and let the soup cook until the potatoes are tender, about 20-25 minutes.
  5. Once the potatoes are cooked, remove the bay leaf from the pot.
  6. Use an immersion blender or a potato masher to partially blend the soup. This will create a creamy base with some chunks of potato remaining.
  7. Stir in the milk and heavy cream. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
  8. Let the soup simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  9. Serve the spicy potato soup hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.
